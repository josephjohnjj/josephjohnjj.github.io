About Me

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology. My research interest is in the area of Parallel Computing with special emphasis on High Performance Computing. I have been working with OpenMP and MPI for quite some time. I have also given training in OpenMP in multiple technical symposiums. At present, I am involved in a project that sets up an HPC system in Sunya Labs at Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology, with a computing power of 6.3 TFLOPS. In relation to this, I have worked with sNow! HPC software suite provided by HPCNow. My work also involves EasyBuild and Intel toolchain.
